# Lewis Howes’ ‘School of Greatness’ Podcast Coming to Tubi Under SiriusXM Three-Year Deal Renewal

- **Backup Date:** 2026-06-19 06:50:52

![Cover Image](https://variety.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Lewis-Howes-School-of-Greatness.png)

## Article Content

![Article Image](https://variety.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Lewis-Howes-School-of-Greatness.png?w=1000&h=667&crop=1)

SiriusXM inked a three-year renewal for Lewis Howes’ self-improvement podcast, “The School of Greatness,” which will expand the show’s distribution on new platforms including Fox Corp.’s Tubi streaming service.

Under the new agreement, “The School of Greatness” will continue to be distributed on the SiriusXM Podcast Network. SiriusXM Media, the company’s advertising division, retains exclusive ad sales rights to the video and audio editions of the podcast. The renewed partnership will also expand the show’s reach to additional platforms, including Tubi.

Said Howes, “Every conversation on this show is about helping someone take the next step toward their greatest self. Expanding our partnership with SiriusXM means that message reaches more people, on more platforms, than ever before. And it feels like we’re just getting started.”

Howes is the author of “The School of Greatness,” “The Mask of Masculinity,” “The Greatness Mindset” and “Make Money Easy.” A former pro football player and two-sport All-American, Howes is the owner of the L.A. Handball Club, where he is “chasing his dream” of competing at the 2028 Summer Olympic Games in his home city of Los Angeles with USA Men’s National Handball Team.
