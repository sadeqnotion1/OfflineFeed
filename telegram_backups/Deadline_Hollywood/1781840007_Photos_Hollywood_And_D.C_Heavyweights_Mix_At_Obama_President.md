- **Original URL:** https://deadline.com/gallery/obama-library-opening-photos-celebrities/

![Cover Image](https://deadline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Barrack.jpg)

## Article Content

![Article Image](https://deadline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Barrack.jpg?w=681&h=383&crop=1)

The biggest names in Hollywood and D.C. mixed today at the Obama Presidential Center opening. Four living former presidents (besides the current one) and their wives were in attendance. Of course, that meant another Altoids moment between Michelle Obama and George Bush.

Also in attendence were Oprah, Steven Spielberg, Tom Hanks, Bono, Bruce Springsteen, Stevie Wonder, David Letterman, Stephen Colbert, Jennifer Hudson, Christina Aguilera, Tyler Perry and many more.

Scroll through the gallery below to see photos of them all.
